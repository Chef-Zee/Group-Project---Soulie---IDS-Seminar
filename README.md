# Soullie — AI Emotional Wellness Companion

## 🧠 Overview
Soullie is an AI-powered emotional wellness platform designed to support students and young professionals experiencing stress, burnout, or loneliness.

It provides a private, self-guided space where users can reflect on their emotions, gain insights, and receive supportive recommendations in real time.

---

## 🎯 Problem
Many people experience emotional overload but lack accessible daily support.

- Therapy is expensive and scheduled
- Most apps focus on only one feature (journaling OR meditation)
- Users need continuous, personalized emotional guidance

---

## 💡 Solution
Soullie combines multiple features into one AI-driven platform:

- ✍️ AI journaling  
- 🧠 Emotional insights  
- 💬 Real-time support  
- 🎧 Mood-based recommendations  

---

## 👥 Target Users
- Students  
- Young professionals  
- Early-career workers  

Example persona:  
Emma, a 24-year-old graduate student feeling overwhelmed and looking for simple, private tools to manage her emotions.

---

## 🚀 Features
- Emotional check-in system  
- Personalized feedback  
- Reflection and growth tracking  
- Calm and supportive UI experience
